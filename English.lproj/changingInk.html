Changing Ink

When the sample raster printer runs out of virtual ink, it is necessary to change the ink from the printer utility. To open the printer utility and change the ink:

  1. Choose "System Preferences" from the Apple menu
  2. Click on "Print & Fax"
  3. Double-click on the sample raster printer
  4. Click on the "Utility" button in the toolbar
  5. Click on the "Change Inks" button
